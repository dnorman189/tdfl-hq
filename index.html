Constitution quick reference

Condensed from the TDFL Constitution (updated 9/30/2024). The full document governs; this is the cheat sheet.

Money

Buy-in (2025 on)$100 Champion (63%)$750 Runner-up (25%)$300 Third place (4%)$50 Reg-season high score (8%)$100

Cap & contracts

  • $200 hard cap enforced at the veteran auction draft.
  • Every kept player's salary rises +$5 per season retained.
  • Rookie picks carry fixed salaries: 1.01 $20 → 1.06–1.12 $10; Rd 2 $5; Rd 3 $1.
  • 2026 amendment: $250 cap enforced year-round; FAAB winning bid becomes the player's salary; IR players don't count; auction cap stays $200.
  • Taxi contracts never count against the cap at the start of the year.

Rosters

  • 25 active · 2 IR · 5 taxi. Starters: 1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, 2 FLEX, 1 DEF, 1 PK.
  • Taxi: rookies and 1-year vets only; locks at the regular season; no returning once promoted mid-year.
  • Players lock at their individual kickoff.

Transactions

  • FAAB: $100/year, blind bids, no min/max, ties broken by waiver order; free-for-all after the run.
  • Trades: never voted on — they stand unless collusion is found. No in-week trading after the first NFL kickoff; deadline applies.

Playoffs

  • 6 teams by win %, Points For breaks ties. Top 2 seeds bye Week 15. No re-seeding.

Governance

  • Amendments: 12/12 to apply this year; 7/12 for next season; ties fail. "Current year" runs championship to championship (Amendment 6.1 "Archibald").
  • Commissioner: Dex Norman · Co-Commissioner: Sean Deeks. Mistakes reported within 24 hours.
